ABOUT US

BRING THE
MAGICAL NIGHTS
THROUGH MUSIC

South East Asia largest EDM festival - Sountopia turns
5 this year! Starting from 2015, the festival has been
held anually in the month of November as a two-days
festival until it expanded into a three-days festival in
2017 with more DJs.

The festival uphoalds the value of Unity through music.
A wide spectrum of sub-genres under the dance music
category are given a platform through the multiple stages
in the festival.
